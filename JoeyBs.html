Joey B’s Famous Chili Hub


This is a great example of a small roadside burger joint with great food and service. The patty itself was tender and juicy, but not sloppy. The sauces and condiments added fantastic taste. The burger was not expensive helping the rating of this burger joint even more. On top of all of this the staff talked to all of their customers as if they were friends, making us feel at home.
