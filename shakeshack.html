Shake Shack


Shake Shack, the king of all burger joints. The only thing to peg on this favorite is its location, an exact 15 miles from where we started. Did it matter? A quick 20 minutes on the highway and you have yourself one of the best tasting burgers this side of the Mississippi. Thick patties, a wide array of options, impeccable service (the buzzers were a nice touch), and literally the best tasting burger we have tried. The patties are cooked to its signature well-done, but its juiciness and tenderness rival those of medium-rare patty. The thickness, the buns, the bacon (best bacon as well), culminate into a burger that is worth the drive. This place is Five Guys top competitor, and although it was close, Shake Shack was the overall winner.
