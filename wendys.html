Wendy's


This is the final joint of the three biggest burger joints in the country. Wendy’s is the most expensive of the three and it follows the same trend. It was the thickest and best tasting burger out of the big three. The fries were above average and so was the staff. The food came out quickly in what was a clean and well-lit building. The toppings seemed to be the tastiest as Wendy’s as the bacon is so thick and flavorful.
