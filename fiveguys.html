Five Guys


This may be the best chain burger joint in the Northeast. The burger itself was tender although not too juicy. You can ask for any topping in the world to be added to your burger. The best part about Five Guys, may not be the burger, but the copious amount of fries that they give you. The staff always doubles the amount you ask for. Five Guys is another restaurant with great staff. Making our burgers to order and the wait being less than five minutes. The burger was slightly overpriced, but you will never leave this place feeling hungry.
