Ginger Man


We went into Ginger Man with high expectations and we left extremely happy. The burger was perfectly juicy and tender, with the chicken cooking every burger exactly how we order it. The sauces that the chef had marinated the tender meat in brought out the true genius of this proud man. The fries left us wanting more as they were perfectly salted. Every topping we asked for on the burger not only added taste but made the burger even more pleasing to the eye. The ambiance was top notch. With attentive service and great food the Ginger Man earned one of the highest rankings out of the burger joints we rated.
