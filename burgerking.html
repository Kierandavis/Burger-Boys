Burger King


Burger King is more expensive than McDonald’s. That means its burgers are thicker, better, and you leave without the stomachache. The fries were just average and so was the staff. The place we visited was run down and dark. We entered Burger King with the expectation that it would be below average but better than McDonald’s and that’s what the result was.
