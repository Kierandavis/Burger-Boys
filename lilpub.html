Little Pub


Little Pub was one of the more fancier restaurants throughout our stops. Despite the crowd throughout the day, Little Pub offered unrivaled ambiance, average service, and a quality burger. Although a bit pricey, the burgers had respective depth, a wide range of options, however all of our burgers weren’t cooked to our exact specifications. Although it didn’t matter too much, it was still a bit disappointing as we had high hopes for this establishment. Nevertheless, the burgers were tasty, the sides were plentiful, and the experience was formidable. If you can beat the crowd, Little Pub is a worthy stop for an upscale eatery.
