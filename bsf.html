Burgers Shakes and Fries


This is the spot to eat in Greenwich. With an extensive menu and over the top service BSF has the best burgers in town. The patties were juicy and flavorful leaving us all begging for more. BSF owner decided to do something bold. He switched buns with butter grilled bread. This was the pièce de résistance. Although the seating was limited, the food did more than enough to make up for only blemish on BSF fantastic burger joint. There is nothing more to be said but to venture out to this heavenly place and taste God in burger form.
